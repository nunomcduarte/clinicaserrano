# Reference — Space: Training Room

**Format:** Single-image Instagram post, 4:5 portrait
**Function:** Atmospheric / space introduction — shows the dedicated training room
**Template followed:** `templates/space-photo-post.md`

## Caption

> *Criámos um espaço de treino, onde tens a privacidade necessária para te focares a 100% na tua recuperação ou performance.*

> ⚠️ **Legacy form.** This caption uses `tu` (`tens`, `te focares`, `tua`), which is now retired from the brand's voice. The canonical Register B form is `você`-imperative (see `voice/tone.md` and `CHANGELOG.md` ADR-0003). On any refresh, the caption should be rewritten as: *"Criámos um espaço de treino, onde tem a privacidade necessária para se focar a 100% na sua recuperação ou performance."*

## Image notes

- Subject: a wide view of the clinic's gym room. Wooden cabinetry with LED-strip lit shelves; concrete-style Nike dumbbells displayed on lit shelves; folded navy `SERRANO CLÍNICA` towels (stacked variant) in the center; Nike weight plates mounted on the side walls — black on the left wall, color-coded smaller plates (red, blue, yellow, green, black) on the right; a red-trimmed adjustable bench in the foreground; black rubber gym flooring.
- Light: strong LED-strip lighting throughout the shelving — the warm glow is the photo's subject.
- Composition: symmetric, full-room frame, centered on the central shelving column.
- The space reads premium-clinical-gym, not commercial-gym.

## What this post established for the system

- Confirms a **dedicated training room exists** within the clinic — supports the `Treino Personalizado` and `Medicina Desportiva` services with a real space.
- ~~Confirms **Register B** can use informal `tu`.~~ **Reversed:** see `CHANGELOG.md` ADR-0003. The brand consolidates Register B around `você`-imperative; this caption's `tu` form is a legacy artifact.
- Reinforces the brand interior aesthetic: warm wood + LED-strip light + cream surfaces + navy/black accents + greenery.
- Reinforces the in-frame branding pattern: navy `SERRANO CLÍNICA` towels are visible without being staged as the subject.

## Open questions raised

- The Nike branding is heavily visible (plates, dumbbells) — is this a sponsorship, a stylistic choice, or just available equipment? Brand co-occurrence is a meaningful signal worth disclosing or formalizing.
- Whether the gym is shot from any other angle for variety, or always the same hero frame.
