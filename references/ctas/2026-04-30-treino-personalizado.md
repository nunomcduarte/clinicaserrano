# Reference — Service CTA: Treino Personalizado (dumbbell close-up)

**Format:** Single-image Instagram post, 4:5 portrait
**Function:** Service-led conversion post — drives bookings for personalized training assessments
**Template followed:** TBD `templates/service-cta-post.md` — this post and the gym-space post together establish that template

## Caption

> *Na nossa clínica, o treino é totalmente personalizado. Ajustamos a carga, a técnica e o plano de treino às suas necessidades e objetivos, garantindo que evolui com segurança e sem lesões. Cada passo é acompanhado por profissionais de saúde, com rigor, para que alcance resultados reais. Comece hoje mesmo e dê ao seu corpo o cuidado que ele merece. Marque já a sua avaliação!*

## Image notes

- Subject: three concrete-textured Nike dumbbells (numbers visible: 10, 12.5) with chrome handles, arranged on a textured cream surface. One dumbbell laid on its side as a compositional break.
- Background: warm wood-grain surface, out of focus.
- Light: soft warm light from upper left.
- Tight, shallow-DoF close-up. The equipment is treated as still life — the materiality of the concrete dumbbell heads is the visual hook.

## What this post established for the system

- Confirms **Register B** (formal `você`/imperative — "Comece", "dê", "Marque") for service CTAs. Note: stays inside `você` throughout — does **not** drift to `tu` like the gym-space post does. This is the cleaner version of Register B.
- Confirms one exclamation per CTA post, placed on the final imperative.
- Establishes the **service CTA caption pattern** in three beats:
  1. What the service is / what makes it personalized.
  2. Who supports / how it's delivered.
  3. Direct call to action.
- Establishes the **equipment-as-still-life** photo treatment as a brand-acceptable surface — the close-up earns its keep through composition and material, not branding overlay.
