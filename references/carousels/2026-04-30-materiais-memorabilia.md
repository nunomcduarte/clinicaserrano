# Reference — Carousel: Materiais & Peças

**Format:** 3-slide Instagram carousel, 4:5 portrait
**Function:** Behind-the-scenes / brand-story — the clinic's material choices and memorabilia
**Template followed:** `templates/space-photo-post.md` (extended to carousel format) — the still-life caption pattern over multiple images

## Caption

> *Desde os materiais que escolhemos para a reabilitação até às peças que contam histórias nas nossas paredes, tudo na Serrano Clínica respira dedicação.*

A single, restrained two-clause caption tying disparate visual subjects to one idea (`dedicação`).

> ⚠️ Legacy form. The caption uses the now-deprecated inversion `Serrano Clínica` — the canonical name is `Clínica Serrano` (see `voice/tone.md` and `CHANGELOG.md` ADR-0001). On any refresh, rewrite as: *"…tudo na Clínica Serrano respira dedicação."*

## What this carousel established for the system

- **The CR7 / Nike connection is intentional and curated** — see `brand/07-space-storytelling.md`. This is brand-defining context for marketing positioning.
- **Materials and memorabilia are first-class brand subjects** — the caption explicitly ties them to dedication / care. Future posts can use objects in the space as legitimate subjects.
- **Multi-image carousel for atmospheric content** — the space-photo template extends naturally to a carousel when there are multiple objects to show under one thematic spine.
- **Caption discipline** — one short caption can carry a whole carousel of disparate images, as long as the spine is clear.

## Open questions raised (high priority)

- **Other framed objects** in the clinic — is this part of a larger "wall of stories"?
