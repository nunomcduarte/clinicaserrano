# Reference — Carousel: Época da Gripe

**Format:** 3-slide Instagram carousel, 4:5 portrait
**Function:** Seasonal announcement — flu season urgent care availability
**Template established:** `templates/instagram-announcement-carousel.md`

## Caption

> *Com a chegada da época da gripe, aumentam os sintomas e as dúvidas sobre onde recorrer rapidamente.*
>
> *Para que nunca fique sem resposta, estamos disponíveis para consultas urgentes e consultas ao domicílio, garantindo acesso rápido, avaliação segura e cuidados adequados ao seu caso.*
>
> *Cuidamos de si quando mais precisa!*

## Slide transcript

### Slide 1 — Cover

- Wordmark: top-center.
- Headline (centered): **ÉPOCA / DA GRIPE:** (two lines, all-caps, large)
- Subhead (centered): **ESTAMOS AQUI PARA / CUIDAR DE SI** (two lines, all-caps, smaller, regular weight)
- CTA pill (centered, low): `Consulta Urgente | Consulta ao Domicílio`

### Slide 2 — Body

- Top-right setup paragraph: *Com a chegada da época da gripe, aumentam os sintomas e as dúvidas sobre onde recorrer.*
- Mid-left reinforcement paragraph: *Por isso, reforçamos que temos disponibilidade para **Consultas Urgentes e ao Domicílio**, garantindo:*
- Bullet list:
  - *Acesso rápido*
  - *Avaliação orientada por profissionais de saúde*
  - *Cuidados personalizados ao seu caso*
- Wordmark: bottom-center.

### Slide 3 — Closing

- Headline (top-left): **A sua saúde não espera, / nós também não.** (sentence case, two lines, large)
- Sub-statement (mid-low, right-aligned): *Agende a sua consulta connosco, / estamos prontos para o ajudar.*
- Wordmark: bottom-center.
- No CTA pill.

## What this carousel established for the system

- The **cream-mode surface variant** (`--color-cream-bg` + `--color-charcoal`) — confirmed.
- The **announcement carousel template** (cover + body + closing-CTA structure).
- The **flexible wordmark position** — top-center on cover, bottom-center on body/closing.
- The **wider CTA pill** with two service labels separated by `|` (a tap-target variant of the swipe-arrow pill).
- The **bold inline emphasis** pattern (slide 2 bolds `Consultas Urgentes e ao Domicílio`).
- The cover headline reads as a cleaner grotesque than the rounded display sans seen elsewhere. **Resolved (`CHANGELOG.md` ADR-0002):** this is an optical effect of all-caps + cream-on-charcoal + tighter tracking — not a second family. The brand uses one sans across all contexts.

## Notable patterns

- The carousel uses **three layouts in three slides** (cover → body → closing) — each visually distinct, each serving a different function. This is a more designed structure than the multi-service overview, where every slide is the same template.
- The cream surface reads notably more "alert/urgent" than the greige despite using the same restraint. The brand has discovered it can signal urgency without sacrificing minimalism — this is a real asset and worth preserving.
- The closing slide uses a **personified contrast** ("A sua saúde não espera, nós também não.") — rare in this brand which is usually more impersonal. Worth flagging in voice doc as a permitted device for closing CTAs in announcement contexts.

## Open questions raised

- Is the cream surface a deliberate variant, or was this carousel built off-system? If deliberate, it should be promoted from "provisional" to "documented" in colors.md.
- Whether the brand wants to standardize on the `Service A | Service B` pill format for any post that surfaces multiple offerings at once.
