# Reference — Philosophy: "Antes do tratamento, começamos por ouvir"

**Format:** Single-image Instagram post, 4:5 portrait
**Function:** Philosophy / values declaration — articulates the clinic's approach to consultation
**Caption pattern:** Pattern 4 (philosophy) in `voice/tone.md`

## Caption

> *Antes do tratamento, começamos por ouvir.*
>
> *Na Serrano Clínica, acreditamos que um bom diagnóstico começa com atenção aos detalhes e tempo dedicado a cada utente.*
> *Aqui, não tratamos apenas sintomas, cuidamos de pessoas.*
> *Cada utente merece tempo, atenção e acompanhamento personalizado.*
>
> *Marque a sua consulta*

## Image notes

- Subject: an older bald clinician with round wire glasses, in a white lab coat with `SERRANOCLÍNICA` embroidery (visible on chest pocket). Mid-conversation, warm smile, eyes engaged.
- Foreground: out-of-focus back of a patient's head (dark hair, partial silhouette) — establishes the consultation context without showing the patient's identity.
- Background: sheer white curtains, soft daylight diffusing through. Warm, calm ambient palette.
- Light: window light from camera-left, very soft. Cinematic backlit framing.
- Frame: vertical, mid-shot. Composition cuts off at chest level.

## What this post established for the system

- A **second senior clinician** in the team — older, possibly Dr. Serrano Sr. or another partner. Name not given in this post; **needs identification** before any future post about this team member is written.
- The **listen-before-treat** principle as the brand's stated consultation philosophy. This is the most explicit values articulation observed so far. Worth treating as a brand pillar.
- Confirms the **multi-paragraph philosophy caption format**:
  - Single-line opening (the headline statement)
  - 2–3 short body paragraphs
  - Single-line closing CTA, calm imperative, no exclamation ("Marque a sua consulta", not "Marque já!")
- Confirms photographic treatment for consultation/candid scenes: protect patient identity by framing them out-of-focus and from behind; let the clinician carry the frame.
- Note: caption uses **"Serrano Clínica"** (inverted form). This is now a deprecated legacy artifact — the canonical name is `Clínica Serrano` (see `voice/tone.md` and `CHANGELOG.md` ADR-0001). On any refresh of this content, the inversion should be rewritten.

## Vocabulary additions

New brand-vocabulary words/phrases observed:

- `utente` — used twice. PT-PT term for "user / patient / client" in healthcare. The brand prefers `utente` over `paciente` or `cliente`. Worth adding to the vocabulary table in `voice/tone.md`.
- `cuidamos de pessoas` — care for people, not symptoms. A reusable brand phrase.
