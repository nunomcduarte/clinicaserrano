# Wordmark

Clínica Serrano uses a wordmark — no symbol or icon has been observed in this system.

## Anatomy

```
SERRANO CLÍNICA
└──┬──┘ └──┬──┘
   │       └──── Display sans, all caps, light weight, slightly wider tracking, optically matched cap height
   └──────────── Didone serif, all caps, regular weight, normal tracking
```

The two halves sit on the same baseline with no separator. They read as one unit, but the typographic contrast (serif × sans, dense × light) gives the mark its character.

The serif portion (`SERRANO`) carries the weight; `CLÍNICA` reads as a quiet qualifier.

## Placement

The wordmark position is **contextual, not fixed.** It sits where it best balances the slide. Across the system observed so far, four positions are in use:

| Position | When to use | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| **Top-right** | Service-introduction slides (multi-service overview) | The 8-slide services carousel — every slide |
| **Top-center** | Cream-mode announcement covers — sets a stately, posterlike feel | Gripe carousel slide 1 |
| **Bottom-center** | Cream-mode announcement body/closing slides — the wordmark signs off the message | Gripe carousel slides 2–3 |
| **Bottom-left** | Greige-mode deep-dive carousels (when paired with a CTA arrow bottom-right) | Exame Médico Desportivo carousel |

Margins from edges are roughly **6–8% of the shorter canvas dimension**, regardless of position.

### Rules of thumb

- **One position per carousel.** Don't move the wordmark around inside the same carousel — pick a placement and hold it across all slides.
- **Color follows the surface.** Cream wordmark on greige paper; charcoal wordmark on cream paper.
- **Size stays small.** The wordmark cap height is ~15–20% of the headline cap height. The wordmark is never the focal element — the headline always is.
- **Wordmark and CTA pill don't share a corner.** If the CTA pill lives bottom-right, the wordmark goes bottom-left or top.

## Don't

- Don't recolor.
- Don't stack the two words vertically.
- Don't put the wordmark on a contrasting plate or box.
- Don't separate `SERRANO` and `CLÍNICA` with a dot, slash, or pipe.
- Don't use either word alone as a logo. The mark is the pair.
- Don't increase the size to compete with the headline.
