# Brand Applications

Physical surfaces where the wordmark and palette are already applied. Documenting these so future merchandise, uniforms, and collateral stay consistent.

## Lab coats

- **Garment:** standard white clinician lab coat, classic cut.
- **Mark:** the full wordmark `SERRANOCLÍNICA` (read as one unit), embroidered on the **left chest, above the pocket**.
- **Thread color:** dark — reads charcoal/near-black on the white fabric.
- **Size:** small. Width roughly the same as the chest pocket opening.
- **Style:** low-contrast, restrained. The mark is recognized, not announced.

Worn by clinical staff (doctors observed).

## Towels

- **Item:** rolled hand towels displayed on shelving in treatment areas.
- **Color:** deep navy (see `01-colors.md` for the auxiliary token).
- **Mark:** the wordmark `SERRANO` (serif) over `CLÍNICA` (sans), **stacked on two lines**, in a cream/off-white embroidery or print.
- This is the only place where the wordmark has been observed stacked. On every other surface it's set on a single line. The stacked version exists because the towel surface is short and wide.

## T-shirts

- **Garment:** cream/off-white short-sleeve crew tee, relaxed fit.
- **Mark:** the wordmark on a single line, **centered horizontally on the chest**, just below the neckline.
- **Print color:** charcoal, low-contrast against the cream fabric.
- **Size:** small — the mark sits modestly, treated more like a luxury-brand label than a sport-team chest print.

Worn by non-clinical staff (e.g. the midwife / IBCLC consultant observed in a t-shirt rather than a lab coat). Worth confirming whether the lab coat vs. t-shirt distinction maps to clinical vs. allied-health roles.

## Common rules across applications

Pulled from the three observed garments, useful when commissioning new merch:

- **Mark is always small.** No oversized chest prints, no full-back prints.
- **Mark is always one of two colors:** cream/ink on dark surfaces; charcoal on cream/white surfaces. No brand color on brand color.
- **Mark is always set in the wordmark's own typography** (Didone serif + light sans). Never substituted with a flat sans.
- **Surfaces are calm:** white, cream, navy. No prints, no patterns, no contrast bands.
